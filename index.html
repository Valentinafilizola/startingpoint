Phases of the event

A night mapped like a recipe card.

The flow is simple: arrive, cook, eat, and stay for whatever the room becomes. This section is intentionally calm so the content feels clear, warm, and easy to scan.

01 arrive
🥄

Sleeves up, hands in

You walk into a kitchen, not a waiting room. We cook together — a recipe rooted in someone's culture, shared openly, made by everyone.

02 gather
🍽️

Eat what you made

Sit down at the table. The conversation that happens when you've built something together with your own hands is different: easier, warmer, more real.

03 create
🎶

The space transforms

After dinner, instruments appear, crafts come out, and art happens. The room becomes whatever the evening calls for.

04 return
🌱

Come back

Your first session is free. Always. After that, membership costs nothing — just your presence. Stay, grow, and bring someone with you next time.

About our chefs

People who lead with flavor, patience, and care.

Our chefs are community teachers. They bring recipes, stories, techniques, and a way of hosting that makes nervous first-timers feel like regulars.

M

Marisol Vega

Teaches masa, salsas, and the art of making a crowded table feel calm.

A

Amina Noor

Leads spice-forward dinners rooted in Somali family recipes and generous storytelling.

J

Jun Park

Builds joyful, precise workshops around fermentation, rice, and shared rituals.

T

Talia Brooks

Creates plant-forward comfort food nights with accessible techniques for every cook.

How they teach

No demos from a distance.

01

Everyone touches the recipe

Chopping, stirring, tasting, adjusting, plating. Nobody watches from the sidelines.

02

Stories are ingredients

Every recipe carries memory, culture, and care. The chefs make room for all of it.

03

Confidence over perfection

The goal is not restaurant polish. The goal is leaving more capable than you arrived.
