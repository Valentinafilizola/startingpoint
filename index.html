Our mission
01

Food insecurity doesn't mean people need
less — it means they've been given less.

Starting Point starts in the kitchen — but that's never really where it ends. When you cook alongside someone, you share recipes, techniques, stories, and a little bit of yourself. By the time you sit down to eat, something has already shifted.

After dinner, the space transforms. Instruments come out. Crafts appear. Someone starts a song. Someone else finishes it. This is what community actually feels like.

The experience

What actually happens

Every session follows a simple arc — but what unfolds inside that arc is different every time, because the people are.

01
🥄

Sleeves up, hands in

You walk into a kitchen, not a waiting room. We cook together — a recipe rooted in someone's culture, shared openly, made by everyone.

02
🍽️

Eat what you made

Sit down at the table. The conversation that happens when you've built something together with your own hands — it's different. Easier. More real.

03
🎶

The space transforms

After dinner, something shifts. Instruments appear. Crafts come out. Art happens. Whatever the evening calls for — music, making, moving — you belong here.

04
🌱

Come back

Your first session is free. Always. After that, membership costs nothing — just your presence. Stay, grow, bring someone with you next time.

What we stand for

Five words
we mean.

These aren't aspirations posted on a wall. They're the reason we design every session the way we do — to stretch people just enough, hold them just enough, and get out of the way.

🌱

Growth

Not comfortable growth. Real growth — the kind that requires you to try a recipe you've never made in a kitchen you don't own yet.

✦

Fulfillment

The feeling of feeding people something you made with your own hands. That's not small. We want everyone to know it.

🌊

Discomfort

We design for stretch. If something feels a little hard, that's not a flaw in the experience — that's the experience.

🔥

Passion

For food. For art. For each other. Passion is contagious and we let it spread freely.

🤝

Connection

You cannot cook with someone and stay a stranger. It's almost impossible. We count on that.

Access & Membership

Open doors.
Free to all.

We will never charge anyone to belong here. Your first session is on us — walk in, cook, eat, make art. No card. No catch. After that, membership is required (it keeps our community intentional) but it's always free.
